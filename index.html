One Piece

Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the main protagonist of the One Piece series. He is the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and is known as the "Fifth Yonko" of the sea. Luffy's main goal is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure known as "One Piece".

Zoro Roronoa

Zoro Roronoa is a fictional character in the One Piece franchise created by Eiichiro Oda. He is the first member of the Straw Hat Pirates to join Luffy's crew, and he serves as the group's swordsman. Zoro is also one of the top three strongest fighters in the crew, along with Luffy and Sanji.
